Our Luxury Packages

Standard Package

Perfect for regular upkeep and maintaining your vehicle’s shine.

Includes:

  • Exterior hand wash & dry.
  • Wheel & tire cleaning.
  • Interior vacuum.
  • Window cleaning. (Inside & out)
  • Interior wipe-down.
  • Tire shine application.

1-1.5hrs duration.

Recommended every 2 weeks.

Premium Package

Ideal for vehicles needing deeper cleaning and added protection.

Includes:

  • Exterior hand wash & dry.
  • Wheel & tire cleaning.
  • Interior vacuum.
  • Window cleaning. (Inside & out)
  • Deep interior cleaning. (Vents, dashboard, cup holders)
  • Tire shine application.
  • Clay bar treatment. (Removes surfaces contaminants)
  • Hand wax or paint sealant.
  • Door jambs cleaned & protected.
  • Leather & vinyl conditioning.
  • Trim & plastic restoration.

2-3hrs duration.

Recommended every 1–2 months.

Elite Package

The ultimate detailing experience for luxury and perfection.

Includes:

  • Exterior hand wash & dry.
  • Wheel & tire cleaning.
  • Interior vacuum.
  • Window cleaning. (Inside & out)
  • Deep interior cleaning. (Vents, dashboard, cup holders)
  • Tire shine application.
  • Clay bar treatment. (Removes surfaces contaminants)
  • Hand wax or paint sealant.
  • Door jambs cleaned & protected.
  • Leather & vinyl conditioning.
  • Trim & plastic restoration.
  • Full paint decontamination & polish. (Gloss enhancement)
  • Advanced paint sealant.
  • Engine bay cleaning & dressing.
  • Steam cleaning of carpets and upholstery.
  • Headlight restoration.
  • Premium interior fragrance.

3-4hrs duration.

Recommended every 3–6 months or for special occasions.
